# Brief — Autism Mom Club

## Positioning
A community platform for moms of autistic children to share recipes, routines, sensory strategies, and small wins that made a real difference for their kid. Mom-focused by design — warm, practical, peer-to-peer.

## Scope (Phase 1)
Static frontend prototype. No backend, no CMS, no user accounts. Submissions handled via embedded Tally or Typeform → spreadsheet for manual curation. Migrate to CMS only after submission volume justifies it.

## Domain
`autismmom.club` (Confirm trademark and social handle availability on Instagram and TikTok before final lockup).

## Visual Direction
- Base: warm off-white `#FAFAF7`, secondary light grey `#F2F2EE`, borders `#E5E5E0`
- Text: near-black `#1A1A1A`
- Accent: neurodiversity-inspired rainbow gradient (pink → orange → yellow → green → teal → soft blue), desaturated ~25% from the Neurosage reference, warmed slightly. Used sparingly — logo, one primary CTA per page, section dividers. Never as a background wash.
- Typography: geometric sans for display/headlines (Space Grotesk or similar), humanist sans or soft serif for body (Inter or Source Serif). Avoid all-geometric — reads cold for a parent audience.
- Photography: candid real-parent imagery. No stock, no sad-kid tropes.
- **Hard do-nots:** no puzzle piece iconography (Autism Speaks association), no red-dominant palettes, no pastel-wellness-blog clichés.

## Core Content Categories
1. Food (texture, safe foods, sensory-friendly recipes)
2. Routines (mornings, transitions, bedtime, meltdowns)
3. Sensory tools and spaces
4. Communication (AAC, scripts, shutdown vs. meltdown)
5. Product reviews (honest, non-affiliate)
6. School and services (IEPs, providers, insurance navigation)

*Note: Tag all content by child's age band and support-needs level — strategies vary significantly across the spectrum and generic "autism" content fails users.*

## Editorial Stance (non-negotiable)
Platform covers quality-of-life, coping, and connection. No cure claims, no unproven biomedical protocols, no MMS/chelation/ivermectin content, no supplement affiliate pushes. Submissions screened against this standard before publication.
